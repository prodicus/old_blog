About Me


Hey, I'm Tasdik, a 19 year old IT Undergrad at SRM University. Currently, I am working in SRM Search Engine as a Sysadmin. My current projects include building a restaurant recommender which recommends you restaurants based on the food you type and the place you are currently in. Servers are my second love and I don't hesistate in breaking down and building it from ground up.

Throughout the years as an undergraduate student, I've worked on projects in several different fields - Information Retrieval, Recommendation systems, Data Science and robotics. During my sophomore year, I was introduced to the world of Open source and I have never looked back since.

In my spare time, I try out new stuff as I believe Intelligence boils down to curiosity. I am a huge advocate of open-source development and a firm believer in John Resig's write code every day. While commit streaks on github do not equate to the skill of a developer, they help keep my skills sharp while I work on the every day projects that I love.
